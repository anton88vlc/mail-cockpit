  `2026-05-03` unless billing is fixed. Open the billing page, update the card,
  then rerun the search for a "payment received" receipt.

Needs reply

- [personal] A contractor asked whether Friday still works. Reply with either
  confirmation or a new time; they are waiting on you.

Waiting

- [admin] Government office received documents but has not confirmed status.
  If there is no answer by `2026-05-07`, send a follow-up in the same thread.

FYI/noise

- Routine receipts, product updates, login notices from recognized sessions,
  and newsletters. No action needed.
